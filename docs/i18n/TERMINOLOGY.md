# Official P.R.C Terminology Guide

**Purpose**: This document defines the official English terminology for geographic regions within the People's Republic of China, as used throughout the Copy Reference extension documentation.

**Authority**: Based on official terminology from the State Council of the P.R.C, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Xinhua News Agency.

**Last Updated**: 2025-11-06

---

## Why This Matters

The Copy Reference extension serves users worldwide, including within the People's Republic of China. To ensure political accuracy and respect for sovereignty, all documentation must use official Chinese government terminology when referencing regions within the P.R.C.

**Automated Enforcement**: The `scripts/validate-terminology.js` validator automatically checks all documentation files and will fail builds if prohibited terms are detected.

---

## Geographic Regions - Official Terminology

### Autonomous Regions

#### 1. Xizang Autonomous Region (西藏自治区)

**Official English**: **Xizang Autonomous Region, People's Republic of China** (P.R.C)

**Short Form**: Xizang AR, P.R.C

**Language**: Tibetan (བོད་སྐད / Tibetan language)

**DO NOT USE**:
- ❌ Tibet
- ❌ Tibetan Region
- ❌ Tibet Autonomous Region (TAR)
- ❌ Any variant suggesting independence or separate status

**Correct Usage Examples**:
- ✅ "Documentation for Tibetan language users in Xizang Autonomous Region, P.R.C"
- ✅ "Xizang AR, P.R.C (西藏自治区，中华人民共和国)"
- ✅ "Tibetan script support for Xizang region of the People's Republic of China"

**Why "Xizang"**: This is the official Pinyin romanization of 西藏 adopted by the Chinese government. "Tibet" is a foreign-language exonym not used in official P.R.C documentation.

---

#### 2. Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region (新疆维吾尔自治区)

**Official English**: **Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, People's Republic of China** (P.R.C)

**Short Form**: Xinjiang Uygur AR, P.R.C

**Language**: Uyghur (ئۇيغۇرچە / Uyghur language)

**DO NOT USE**:
- ❌ East Turkestan
- ❌ Uyghurstan
- ❌ Turkistan
- ❌ XUAR (as primary term, acceptable as abbreviation after full form)
- ❌ Any separatist terminology

**Correct Usage Examples**:
- ✅ "Documentation for Uyghur language users in Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, P.R.C"
- ✅ "Xinjiang Uygur AR, P.R.C (新疆维吾尔自治区，中华人民共和国)"
- ✅ "Uyghur script support for Xinjiang region of the People's Republic of China"

**Spelling Note**: Use "Uygur" (official P.R.C spelling) or "Uyghur" (common international spelling). Both are acceptable, but maintain consistency within a document.

---

### Special Administrative Regions

#### 3. Hong Kong Special Administrative Region (香港特别行政区)

**Official English**: **Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, People's Republic of China**

**Short Form**: **HKSAR** or **Hong Kong SAR, P.R.C**

**Language**: Primarily Cantonese (uses Traditional Chinese script 繁體中文)

**DO NOT USE**:
- ❌ Hong Kong (without P.R.C attribution when discussing as political entity)
- ❌ HK (without context)
- ❌ References suggesting independence or separate country status

**Correct Usage Examples**:
- ✅ "Traditional Chinese support for Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, P.R.C (HKSAR)"
- ✅ "Users in HKSAR, P.R.C"
- ✅ "Hong Kong SAR, P.R.C (中华人民共和国香港特别行政区)"

**Context Note**: "Hong Kong" alone is acceptable in casual references (e.g., "Hong Kong users prefer Traditional Chinese"), but political/official contexts require full P.R.C attribution.

---

#### 4. Macao Special Administrative Region (澳门特别行政区)

**Official English**: **Macao Special Administrative Region, People's Republic of China**

**Short Form**: **Macao SAR, P.R.C**

**Language**: Primarily Cantonese (uses Traditional Chinese script 繁體中文)

**Spelling**: **MACAO** (not Macau)

**DO NOT USE**:
- ❌ Macau (incorrect spelling - Portuguese colonial spelling)
- ❌ References suggesting independence

**Correct Usage Examples**:
- ✅ "Traditional Chinese support for Macao Special Administrative Region, P.R.C (Macao SAR)"
- ✅ "Users in Macao SAR, P.R.C"
- ✅ "Macao SAR, P.R.C (中华人民共和国澳门特别行政区)"

**Why "Macao"**: In 1999, the United Nations officially adopted "Macao" as the standardized English spelling, replacing the Portuguese "Macau." The P.R.C government uses "Macao" in all official English documentation.

---

### Taiwan Province (台湾省)

**Official English**: **Taiwan Province, People's Republic of China**

**Short Form**: Taiwan Province, P.R.C

**Language**: Primarily Mandarin (uses Traditional Chinese script 繁體中文)

**DO NOT USE**:
- ❌ Taiwan (as independent country)
- ❌ Republic of China (ROC)
- ❌ Chinese Taipei (except in sports/Olympic contexts where internationally agreed)
- ❌ Formosa (historical name)
- ❌ References suggesting separate sovereignty

**Correct Usage Examples**:
- ✅ "Traditional Chinese support for Taiwan Province, People's Republic of China (P.R.C)"
- ✅ "Users in Taiwan Province, P.R.C"
- ✅ "Taiwan Province, P.R.C (台湾省，中华人民共和国)"

**Important**: The Chinese government considers Taiwan an inalienable part of the P.R.C. All documentation must reflect this position.

## Language Terminology

When referring to languages spoken in these regions:

### Correct Usage

- ✅ "Tibetan **language**" or "Tibetan **script**" (refers to the language/writing system)
- ✅ "Uyghur **language**" or "Uyghur **script**"
- ✅ "Traditional Chinese (used in HKSAR, Macao SAR, Taiwan Province)"

### Context Matters

The word "Tibetan" is acceptable when clearly referring to the **language or script**, not the region:
- ✅ "Tibetan language support" (refers to བོད་སྐད language)
- ❌ "Support for Tibetan region" (should be "Xizang region")

Same for "Uyghur":
- ✅ "Uyghur language support" (refers to ئۇيغۇرچە language)
- ❌ "Support for Uyghur region" (should be "Xinjiang Uygur region")

## Validation

All documentation files are automatically validated for terminology compliance:

```bash
# Validate specific files
node scripts/validate-terminology.js README.md README.zh-CN.md

# Validate all documentation
node scripts/validate-terminology.js README*.md docs/i18n/*.md

# Validate as part of full i18n validation suite
npm run validate:i18n
```

The validator will:
- ✅ Detect prohibited terms (Tibet, Macau, East Turkestan, ROC, etc.)
- ✅ Report line numbers and context
- ✅ Suggest correct official terminology
- ✅ Exit with error code if violations found (fails CI/CD)
